# Play Store listing FAQ (paste into Play Console if supported)

**Q: Is GatePass+ for residents or guards?**  
A: Both. Residents use visitor and maintenance features; guards switch to Guard Mode at the gate.

**Q: How do I join my society?**  
A: Select your society at login and use credentials from your housing society admin.

**Q: Can I pay maintenance in the app?**  
A: Yes, when your society enables online payments (UPI, Razorpay, or PhonePe).

**Q: Is my data safe?**  
A: Yes. Access is role-based and scoped to your society. See our Privacy Policy on the legal site.

**Q: Who should my society contact to get started?**  
A: Email divine.drwa@gmail.com or ask your RWA committee to request onboarding.
