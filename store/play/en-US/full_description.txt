GatePass+ is the all-in-one society management app for residents, security guards, and housing society committees in India.

Whether you live in a gated community, apartment complex, or RWA-managed housing society, GatePass+ helps you approve visitors, pay maintenance, raise complaints, and stay connected — from one premium, easy-to-use app.

━━ FOR RESIDENTS ━━
• Pre-approve guests with OTP or QR — no more waiting at the gate
• Approve or reject walk-in visitors from your phone
• Pay society maintenance online (UPI, Razorpay, PhonePe) or track dues
• See where your maintenance money goes — transparent expense breakdown
• Raise and track complaints
• Read society notices, polls, events & documents
• Book amenities, manage parcels, log vehicles
• SOS alerts for medical, fire & security emergencies
• Community directory & daily help staff

━━ FOR SECURITY GUARDS ━━
• Dedicated guard mode: QR scan, walk-in check-in, delivery & vehicle entry
• Pre-approved visitor list & instant admit
• Patrol logs, incident reports & shift details
• Works even with weak connectivity — offline queue syncs when back online
• Resident directory at the gate

━━ FOR SOCIETY ADMINS (on mobile + web) ━━
Committee members with admin access can manage operations from the Admin tab — while residents use the same app for daily life.

Your society can also use the GatePass+ admin dashboard (web) for billing cycles, expense tracking, gate analytics, UPI verification, reconciliation, and more.

━━ WHY SOCIETIES CHOOSE GATEPASS+ ━━
✓ Built for Indian housing societies & RWAs
✓ Resident + Guard + Admin in one platform
✓ Per-society branding — your society, your look
✓ Secure, role-based access
✓ Transparent maintenance & society finances

━━ GET STARTED ━━
1. Select your society
2. Log in with credentials from your admin
3. Start approving visitors and managing your home
